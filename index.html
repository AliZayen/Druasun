About DronaSun

DRONA’SUN combines advanced drone technology with AI to revolutionize olive farming. By enabling early disease detection and optimizing farming practices, it promotes precision agriculture and sustainability, ensuring healthier orchards and improved yields.

How To Use ?

DRONA’SUN transforms olive farming with advanced drone and AI technology, enabling early disease detection, precise irrigation management, soil health analysis, and targeted pest control. It also generates detailed orchard maps and monitors tree growth to optimize yields. By enhancing productivity and promoting sustainable practices, DRONA’SUN supports eco-friendly and efficient farming.
